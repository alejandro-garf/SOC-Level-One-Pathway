# Humans as Attack Vectors — Writeup

## The Human Element

- **Humans** are considered the weakest link in cybersecurity.
- Attackers target humans to gain **access** to systems and networks.

## Attacks on Humans

- **Social Engineering** — manipulating individuals into divulging information or taking actions.
- **Impersonation** — posing as a trusted entity to deceive targets.

## Defending Humans

- **Mitigation** — implementing controls to reduce human-related risk.
- **Security Awareness Training** — educating users to recognize and respond to threats.
