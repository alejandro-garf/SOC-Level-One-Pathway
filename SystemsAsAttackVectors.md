## Attacks on Systems

Systems can be compromised through several attack vectors:

- **Vulnerabilities** — Weaknesses in software or hardware that can be exploited by attackers.
- **Supply Chain Vulnerabilities** — Compromising a system indirectly by targeting a trusted third-party vendor or software in the supply chain.
- **CVE-2025-53770** — A real-world example of a critical vulnerability; the "ToolShell" SharePoint zero-day allowing unauthenticated remote code execution.
- **Patch Misconfigurations** — Incorrectly applied or missing patches that leave systems exposed even after updates are released.
